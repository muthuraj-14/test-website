Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our student mentor platform.

How do I find a mentor?

+

You can browse our mentor directory, filter based on your academic needs or career goals, and select a mentor who matches your interests.

How do I book a session with a mentor?

+

Once you've selected a mentor, click on the "Book Session" button on their profile. You'll be prompted to choose a time slot and complete the booking.

Can I cancel or reschedule a session?

+

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule a session up to 24 hours before the session time. If you cancel within 24 hours, you may not receive a refund.

Are mentors verified?

+

Yes, all mentors go through a rigorous verification process to ensure they have the qualifications and experience to provide valuable guidance.

How are sessions conducted?

+

Sessions are conducted via video call on our platform. You will receive a link to join the session after your booking is confirmed.

What if I'm not satisfied with a session?

+

If you're not satisfied with the mentorship session, please contact our support team. We'll work with you to either provide a refund or arrange a follow-up session.
